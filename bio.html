Biography

Born 1972, Nigeria

Alfie Nze is a 51-year-old personal trainer who enjoys theatre, badminton and eating out. He is kind and insightful. He is Nigerian. He started studying sports science at college but never finished the course. He is passionate about art in general. Physically, Alfie is in pretty good shape. He is average-height with bronze skin, grey hair and brown eyes. He grew up in a working class neighbourhood. He was raised by his mother, his father having passed away when he was young. He is currently living in Milan Italy where he plies his trade. Alfie has two children he loves dearly. his children pretty much adores him too.
