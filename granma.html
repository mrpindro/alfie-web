A JOURNEY OF NO RETURN

Gran'Ma

An imaginative journey in a land that is too rich, always prey to violent conquests. It is the journey through the historical memory of Alfie Nze, a Nigerian theater director who moved to Italy in the nineties who tells us about two brutal episodes that took place in Nigeria: the bloody British invasion of Benin City in 1897 and the scandal of toxic waste dumped in 1987 in Koko, a village in the Niger Delta.
